01 — Operating Model

How the estate is layered

Not everything is built from scratch. Proven packaged cores carry the systems of record; Lyzr is the agentic experience & orchestration layer that builds the bespoke insurance journeys fast and stitches every system into one governed flow; AWS is the cloud and the security perimeter.

Lyzr — Experience & Orchestration

The build-fast layer. New-business STP, underwriting & claims decisioning, servicing agents, correspondence, portal/app back-end, and one governed SuperFlow across every system.

Packaged Core — Systems of Record

Policy Admin (PAS), General Ledger / Finance, CRM & distribution (Salesforce FSC). Bought and configured, not rebuilt — this is where the timeline is protected.

AWS — Cloud & Guardrails

India-region landing zone, data residency, IRDAI cybersecurity controls, identity, networking. Spend stays metered on AWS.

The discipline that wins the RFP: we do not pretend Lyzr is a policy-admin system. We buy the core, build the differentiated experience on Lyzr in weeks not years, and integrate — so day-one go-live is realistic and the running cost is low.

05 — Non-Negotiable Regulatory Line Items

Built into the plan from day one

  • Ind AS 117 (IFRS 17-equivalent) — mandatory from 1 Apr 2026. PAS + GL + reporting must be Ind AS-native at launch (CSM, annual cohorts).
  • IRDAI Information & Cybersecurity Guidelines 2026 — technical controls, annual external VAPT, vendor-risk program, incident reporting.
  • DPDP Act 2023 + data residency — host in AWS India region; consent architecture; encryption in transit & at rest.
  • ISNP approval — required to sell/service via the insurer's own digital platform; India-hosted, VAPT'd, IRDAI-approved.
  • Bima Sugam — list products & enable servicing/claims via APIs (Aadhaar / DigiLocker / e-IA); phased through 2026 — design for it now.
  • Claims settlement SLA — 30 days (90 with investigation) — baked into the claims flow with audit trail.

07 — Commercial Framing

Why the total cost of ownership is lower than a stacked licence model

Lyzr

One-time build + tokenomics ($5 / 1M tokens; only agent nodes billed, orchestration/RPA nodes free). No AMC — capabilities are baked in.

Packaged cores

PAS, Salesforce, Databricks carry their own vendor licences — separate from Lyzr, sized to the launch scope.

Services & cloud

Accenture professional services (the build) + AWS consumption. The earlier "expensive" read was services markup, not Lyzr pricing.
